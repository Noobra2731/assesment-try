Crop Details & Cultivation Information

Explore comprehensive information about various crops, their growth requirements, and best cultivation practices.

Rice

Rice is a staple food crop grown in warm, humid climates. It requires plenty of water and is typically cultivated in flooded fields called paddies.

  • Soil: Clayey, loamy soil
  • Water: High (flooded fields)
  • Season: Kharif (June–November)

Wheat

Wheat is a major cereal crop grown in cooler climates. It is sown in winter and harvested in spring or early summer.

  • Soil: Well-drained loamy soil
  • Water: Moderate
  • Season: Rabi (October–March)

Maize

Maize, or corn, is a versatile crop used for food, fodder, and industrial products. It grows best in warm temperatures.

  • Soil: Fertile, well-drained soil
  • Water: Moderate
  • Season: Both Kharif and Rabi

Sugarcane

Sugarcane is a tropical crop grown for sugar production. It requires a long, warm growing season and abundant water.

  • Soil: Deep, rich loamy soil
  • Water: High
  • Season: February–March (planting)
