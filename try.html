Common tomato varieties include **Pusa Ruby**, **Arka Rakshak**, and **Roma VF**, suited for different climates and market types.

Nursery-raised seedlings are transplanted after 25 days. Regular watering and organic manure improve fruit quality.

Requires **sandy loam soil** (pH 6‚Äì7) and **warm climate**. Avoid frost or heavy rains during fruiting.
